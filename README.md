Privacy Policy for Auto-Capitalizer
Last Updated: April 23, 2026

At Auto-Capitalizer, we believe that your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles information when you use it on your targeted websites.

1. Information Collection and Use
Auto-Capitalizer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Text Input: The extension processes text as you type it into input fields to convert it to uppercase. This processing happens entirely within your browser on your local device.

No Remote Logging: No text, keystrokes, or website URLs are ever sent to a remote server or third party.

2. Data Storage
The extension uses the chrome.storage.local API (or browser.storage.local for Firefox) to store exactly one piece of information:

The "Enabled" State: A simple "true/false" value that remembers whether you have toggled the extension on or off.
This data is stored locally on your machine and is never synced or shared.

3. Permissions Justification
In accordance with the "Permission Minimization" policy, we only request:

Storage: To remember your "on/off" preference.

Host Permissions: To access the specific website(s) you have chosen for text transformation.

4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. There are no third-party trackers, analytics, or advertisements embedded in this extension.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
